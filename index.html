What ITDS Integrates

Meanline Design

1D thermodynamic design with validated loss correlations, off-design performance, and off-design prediction.

Throughflow

2D radial equilibrium for multi-stage design and performance prediction across the design space.

Geometry Engine

Parametric blade and annulus geometry generation for CAD export, meshing prep, or CFD.

Mesh Automation

Automated grid generation for CFD (ANSYS TurboGrid, Numeca, open-source tools).

CFD Integration

Adapters for ANSYS Fluent, CFX, OpenFOAM. Run CFD, extract validation data, feed back to design.

Extensibility

Plugin architecture for your proprietary models (loss correlations, heat transfer, stress).
